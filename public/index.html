Data and Analytics in Higher Ed

Analytics Developer at SimpsonScarborough

How do we measure and quantify a brand? How do we elevate brand analytics beyond vanity metrics and into actionable strategy? How do we conduct brand and market research ethically?

I have worked with dozens of higher ed institutions to solve for these challenges. Higher Ed marketing and brand research must get smarter. That requires a marriage of technical expertise and thoughtful strategy. It requires us to see the world of data around us, and the know-how to activate it. Data drives more informed insights. Better insights lead to more relevant strategy, and better strategy ultimately provides the best experience for our clients and customers.
